Roleplaying with Sophie

Roleplay is my main creative writing outlet, and probably how you found me, isn't it? Uh huh, thought so!

I don't care much about how much my roleplay partners write, but I do like their writing to be clean and presentable, with good spelling and punctuation. I play in the first person, usually.

I like to GM games sometimes. I'm fond of sandboxes, and I spend a lot of my time I should be working making them (don't tell my boss). One of these days, I'll write a guide on how to play a GM-and-player RP, because not everyone gets it!

I do ERP sometimes. Don't look at me like that! I have needs too, m'kay? I tend to be pretty vanilla, and I'm not into BDSM. If you found me somewhere decidedly not-vanilla, keep it secret, keep it safe.
