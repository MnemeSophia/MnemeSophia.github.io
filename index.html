About Sophie, the Coolest Person

I have lots of hobbies! I like ballroom dance, classic literature, poetry, and I think languages are pretty nifty! I like to crochet, cook, and I'm learning how to make wine! I play videogames and run a tabletop game in the library on Saturday mornings, and at the university on Wednesday nights!

People ask me what I look like, so here's the best I can do: I'm 5'2" and kinda small. I have light brown hair, brown eyes, and a fair complexion. I'm not a huge fan of denim and I hate tight leggings, so I wear skirts and dresses when I can.

Roleplaying with Sophie

Roleplay is my main creative writing outlet, and probably how you found me, isn't it? Uh huh, thought so!

I don't care much about how much my roleplay partners write, but I do like their writing to be clean and presentable, with good spelling and punctuation. I play in the first person, usually.

I like to GM games sometimes. I'm fond of sandboxes, and I spend a lot of my time I should be working making them (don't tell my boss).
