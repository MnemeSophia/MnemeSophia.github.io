Sophie's "Dungeon Crawl" SRD

I have strong opinions about how D&D should be played. Rather than talk your ear off, these are my house rules for 0e. Why 0e? Because it begs to be house-ruled, and is the easiest edition to homebrew from.

Stats

Roll 3d6 and assign the results in order to Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma.

Stat Mod
3-6 -1
7-14 0
15-18 +1

STR: Apply to melee to-hit
DEX: Apply to ranged to-hit
CON: Apply to each HD when rolling HP
INT: Additional spell if caster
WIS: Apply to saving throws
CHA: Apply to reaction rolls and morale
