Cedarvale GM Page

This page is for the GM only. Players turn back.

Here is everything you will need to run the Cedarvale sandbox.

Hex Key

A key to the hexes on the map.

05 Hall of the Great Goblin

The Hall of the Great Goblin, where the creeping things under the mountain are bred for war.

08 Ilien

The Hidden Valley kept by Niriel, the eldest of the fair folk. A haven of art and song, far removed from the troubles of the world.

11 Barrows

The dark barrows of the ancient kings stand under black columns. Haunted, but treasure can be found in the barrows.

13 Cedarvale

A village of hunters and frontier rangers, once the faithful servants of a long-forgotten kingdom. Possible werebear problem.15 Forgotten Altar

A mountaintop altar to an unknown god. Under the new moon, a column of light rises from the center.

24 Old Forest Road

The old road leading through the forest. Once safe. Continues to 34.

25 Witch's Home

The home of Anna the Witch. She's a master of spells that charm animals, and can divine rumors.

26 Standing Stones

A circle of standing stones in the shape of kings and queens. A great giant is burried below with his sword.

27 Necromancer's Tower

The Tower of Thu, the Necromancer, from which proceed the ghosts that haunt the woods.

33 Glathspell

The underground hall of Durigwel, the lord of the Wood Elves. Paranoid, unpredictable.

34 Old Forest Road

The old road leading through the forest. Once safe. Continues to 24 and 35.

35 Spider Den

The dark haven of the Spiders of Shesh. They have grown fat on the souls sent through the woods by Thu. The Old forest road continues to 34 and 46.

37 Well of Souls

An ancient well without bottom, where the souls of the deep can be summoned forth by paying them tribute.

38 Cockatrice Lair

The fields kept by the Cockatrice, the king of fowl.

41 Khavul

The Hall of Grimr, the Masked King of the Dwarves.

43 Wulfsted

The village of the Var, who clad themselves in the pelts of wolves and drink the blood of animals and men.

45 Forest Mansion

An abandoned mansion with prominent eagle motifs. Once the home of a great lord, now the den of vampires.

46 Forest of Shades

Shades loosed by Thu wander these woods, disembodied but by no means harmless. The old forest road continues to 35 and 56.

52 Lion Den

A pride of lions stalks these lands.

53 Ruined Temple

An ancient, crumbling temple to a formless god of the deep.

54 Desert Orc Stronghold

A fortified outpost of orcs from across the desert sands.

56 Kingsroad

Once a great city at the edge of the desert, laid desolate by the coming of Gormund the Wyrm to the Horn. Mostly scorched, with only a village's population inside its crumbling walls.

57 Dragon's Lair

The lair of Gormund the Wyrm, who came over the sands of the desert and ravaged the lands about the Horn.
