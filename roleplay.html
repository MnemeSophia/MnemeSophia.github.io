Roleplaying!

Let's be real, this is why you're here. Let's talk scenarios.

  • Raiders!

    The year is 1936. We're treasure hunters hot on the tail of an ancient artifact. Tomb raiding, shootouts, and daring escapades!

  • Ad Sidera

    Humanity has taken to the stars with the invention of the spike drive, a metadimensional FTL system freeing us from the confines of our planet.

    We're colonists on one of the first interstellar voyages, our mission to establish a new foothold in the universe.

  • Pig at a Wedding

    Alice and Bob are getting married. Ugh.

    They invited us, too. Ugh. They're just the worst.

    We meet before the wedding, and discover out mutual hatred of the bride and groom. Over drinks far too late in the evening, while we commiserate, we hatch a plan. The perfect revenge.

    We're going to sneak a huge fucking pig into the wedding, and set it loose at the perfect time.

    It's going to take discipline. Guts. Ingenuity. But by God, it will be so worth it.
